SEO Blogger Affiliate Marketing 

Introduction

SEO blogger outreach is an approach that involves partnering with top-tier bloggers to post quality articles on their high-quality and trusted blogs. This also boosts your blog rankings and builds link backs for your site. In this way, you can use a blogging platform to help you build links back to your site. The goal of the SEO blogging strategy is to get more high-quality backlinks to your site as you build more authority. You can do this through guest blogging, participating in forums and posting valuable content on the blog.

LA great way to start the SEO blogging process is by identifying keywords that are not only highly relevant to your industry, but that are also hard to dominate within Google. A good SEO blogger will look for these keywords within the text of blog posts and articles. 

Once identified, you can begin to incorporate these keywords within the blog post or article itself. If you find that the keyword is hard to dominate within Google's text, then you may want to consider changing your main keywords to "niche" words that are less competitive. Once you have the keywords that you think are the best for the SEO blogging campaign, you need to determine which bloggers to engage. For example, if you have a niche within blogging that focuses on young children, you may want to engage several younger bloggers.

 This ensures that your audience is diverse and will be interested in your writing. You can easily identify "key bloggers" in your niche through various means.

Once you have found several "key bloggers," the next step is to offer them free gifts in exchange for writing an SEO blog post or article. Many SEO experts offer their readers free eBooks or other information in exchange for blogging. In some cases, you can pay for a simple blog post, but this should be a last resort. If you have established a large audience, and you feel that the blog post or article will be useful to your readers, you may want to consider offering them something in return.

 

Once you have identified several "key bloggers," you can offer them incentives for every new post or article they write. One great way to do this is to offer to give them credit in the blog post or article (you can specify how much in the case of paid blogging) in exchange for them writing an article or posting a blog entry. If the incentives are clear, and the bloggers understand what they are getting into, they will be more likely to take the action you want. If they opt to do something besides writing articles and posting content, you should ask them what they would have liked to have been able to do. For example, if you are offering an ebook or video as a gift, ask the bloggers what they would have like to include in the package. You may even want to create a guest blog for each of them. 

 

Another strategy that many SEO bloggers use is blog directories. Many of these directories let you include your link within the blog posts and allow comments to be added on the page as well.

 By using SEO blogger outreach methods, you can build relationships with other bloggers who can help to promote your links through comments and even blog posts. This can result in increased link popularity and also increase traffic for your site.
